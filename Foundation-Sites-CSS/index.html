Boxing Day Tsunami Juxtapose

The Interactive Timeline shows the differences between the Thailand Coast before and after the Boxing Day Tsunami. The Boxing Day Tsunami was the worst tsunami on record, it misplaced an estimated 1.7 million people and destroyed the land it hit. The tsunami was caused by a 9.1 magnitude earthquake at sea, the tsunami took the lives of over 225,000 people.

Juxtapose of the Olympics

The modern olympics started on April 6th 1896. Now the olympics continue every four years. As sports have evolved there are now Summer and Winter olympics, which are staggered on years, so every two years one of the two Olympic Games are occurring. The timeline gives an understanding of what the games looked like now vs. then.
